Step 2: What is "vw" (Viewport Width)? ‚ÜîÔ∏è

vw means "viewport width" - it's a percentage of how wide your browser window is.

The Math:
  • 1vw = 1% of viewport width
  • 10vw = 10% of viewport width
  • 50vw = 50% of viewport width (half the screen width)
  • 100vw = 100% of viewport width (full screen width)

Step 3: What is "vh" (Viewport Height)? ‚ÜïÔ∏è

vh means "viewport height" - it's a percentage of how tall your browser window is.

The Math:
  • 1vh = 1% of viewport height
  • 25vh = 25% of viewport height (quarter screen height)
  • 50vh = 50% of viewport height (half screen height)
  • 100vh = 100% of viewport height (full screen height)
